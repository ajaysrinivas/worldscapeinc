Shivakumar Mathapathi (Shiva)

COO and GM

Shivakumar a seasoned technology and business executive with over 20 years of experience in the IT, telecom and mobility industries. As COO and General Manager Shiva manages Dew mobility solutions. He is responsible for product development, professional service delivery, operations, customer care and management of the business.

He began his carrier as Design Engineer at Tata Power Company (a division of $ 80 Billion Tata’s group) and rose up to top management position through ranks. Continued as Senior Design Engineer at Rolta – Intergraph, manufacturer of 64 bit RISC clipper based CAD/CAM workstations for International market. Further he was a Regional Customer Engineering Manager with HCL Infosystems (formerly HCL-HP), the largest IT and telecom service provider in the world.  Read More
